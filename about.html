About Me

I am a Computer Science student with a strong focus on web development. I'm passionate about turning creative ideas into functional and accessible digital experiences using modern technologies like React, Next.js, and Tailwind CSS.

Outside coding, I enjoy football, gaming, and experimenting with design tools like Figma to improve my sense of layout and usability.

Goals in This Program

My main goal is to sharpen my problem-solving skills, gain hands-on experience working on real-world projects, and grow into a well-rounded full-stack developer who can handle both frontend and backend development with confidence.

I also want to collaborate with other developers, learn new workflows, and build a strong professional network in the tech ecosystem.

Areas of Low Confidence
